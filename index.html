About Us

RM Lawnmowers & Garden Equipments

RM Lawnmowers is located in Drumconrath Co.Meath. We are a personally owned business, established in 2015, serving customers from Meath, Louth, Cavan, Monaghan

We are main agents for Mountfield lawnmowers, Snapper lawnmowers, Echo power equipment, Tanaka power equipment and Gardencare products. All our quality equipment comes with warranty and parts are readily available, though we will look after your product long after your warranty expires.

We carry a wide range of new Lawn and Garden equipment as well as a broad range of parts in stock all year round. We also stock a wide range of Chainsaws and spare chains, this allows the end user an excellent selection of top quality products, from the professional logger, landscaper, farmer and of course the demanding homeowner.

We will gladly service and repair any make and model from such manufacturers as Stihl, Husqvarna, Echo, Honda, Castle garden, Mountfield, Snapper, Toro, Briggs and Stratton, Kohler and many more.

Over the years, we have gained a great reputation with both domestic and commercial clients for our outstanding work and customer service.We insure each customer is given a full breakdown of the repairs required and the cost to carry out the work to get your equipment working as good as new.
